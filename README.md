# Garanzi 🔒

> **Next-Generation Decentralized Escrow & Payment Infrastructure for Global Freelancing**
> Built on the Stellar Network using Soroban Smart Contracts.

---

## 🌎 Overview

**Garanzi** is a secure, flexible, and automated Web3 escrow platform designed to revolutionize how freelancers and clients collaborate globally. By leveraging the speed and low cost of the Stellar network, Garanzi eliminates payment insecurity, high platform fees, and the risk of client "ghosting." 

Whether it is a quick solo task, a shared team milestone, or an instant performance reward, Garanzi provides cryptographic trust and complete financial automation for the modern decentralized workforce.

## ✨ Core Features

### 1. 💼 Flexible Escrow Contracts
* **One-Time Payments:** Perfect for fixed-scope, short-term assignments. Supports single or shared tasks with instant multi-wallet distributions upon completion.
* **Milestone Payments:** Tailored for complex, long-term projects. Budgets are locked in escrow and released sequentially as each project phase is completed and approved.

### 2. ⚡ Automated Disperse Mechanism (Anti-Ghosting Protection)
* Designed to protect freelancers from unresponsive clients. 
* Once deliverables are submitted, a **48-hour grace period** countdown begins for client review.
* If the client remains inactive or fails to request a dispute within **48 hours**, the smart contract automatically triggers an **`Auto Disperse`** status, releasing the locked funds directly to the freelancers' wallets.

### 3. 🔗 Dynamic Pay Links
* **Escrow Links:** Generates secure links for contract agreements where funds are securely held by the smart contract until conditions or countdowns are met.
* **Bonus Links:** Generates instant reward or tip links that bypass milestones and review periods, sending digital assets directly to a freelancer's wallet.
